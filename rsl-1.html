Rising Sun League Season 1

Rising Sun League (RSL) presented by Rising Sun Esports, sponsored by KIX and LooT, is the first Clash Royale league focused on competitive teams in Japan. Through the competition, top Japanese ladder and competitive players formed over 30 teams and showcased their talent, to prove that they have what it takes to bring their team as the best in Japan. With the conclusion of Season 1, we plan on bringing in even more excitement and talent to our future seasons.
