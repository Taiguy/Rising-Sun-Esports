#RSWin!

Rising Sun Esports is a Japanese Esports Organization established in 2019, with a professional Brawl Stars team and a competitive Clash Royale team. Through our teams, we are expanding and exposing the Japanese community to the world’s competitive esports scene. Rising Sun Esports has already had great success with numerous championships. Our professional Brawl Stars team has showcased their talent in multiple Brawl Stars Championships and other competitions. Our Clash Royale team is representing Japan as the national team in several international Clash Royale league competitions, including the World Cup and World Royale League.
