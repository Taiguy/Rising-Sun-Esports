#RSWin!

Rising Sun Esports is a Japanese Esports organization established in 2019. With a professional Brawl Stars team and a semi-pro Clash Royale team, we have grown into an organization of over 40 members.

Throughout our teams, we are expanding and exposing the Japanese community to the world's competitive Esports scene. We are committed to ensure that every member is held at the highest standard, while providing excellent coaching and player support. With plans for future expansion and success, Rising Sun Esports stands at the forefront of the Japanese Esports community.

Rising Sun Esports has already had great success with numerous championships. Our professional Brawl Stars team has showcased their talent in multiple Brawl Stars Championships, and our Clash Royale team has shown dominance, not only in Japan, but also in Asia and worldwide.
